About Us

Welcome to Plan to Part

Future - Saving - Generation

I live at the Jersey Shore with my husband of 35 years Walter Gabela . My 2 daughters, Jacqueline and Marissa, spend as much time as they can at our beach home. With a BS in Economics from Rutgers University I started my career at Morgan Stanley 1979 where I worked on the equity trading desk. My career as an equity sales trader spanned 30 years working at high profile firms such as UBS and Goldman Sachs. In 2008 during bad economic times I left Wall St and moved to the beach full time. In 2008 my mom died from a tragic accident and my 7 siblings and I had a very difficult time settling the estate. It was not a high net worth estate but it did have financial assets, real estate and personal possession. The family was so distraught from the tragedy that it was extremely difficult to focus on possessions and selling real assets. My sister , Maria Morse , and a co-founder of Plan to Part ,was executrix of the estate and painstakingly worked to balance all the emotions of our siblings and their children. It was at this time that Maria and I knew we did not want this chaos for our families when the time comes. We also realized we did not want traditional funeral parlor arrangements and repasse. It was clear we wanted more of a celebration of life to honor the past and to include as many family and friends in the celebration. As we began our search for a place to arrange our wishes and to store all the information that will be needed for the family, we quickly realized very few online options existed. We also wanted to take it a step further including healthcare, legal , pet care and other important items in the plan. There is also online advice to plan estates and minimize taxation or utilize benefits. As we set off to start this online company we added Lisa Albanese as our third co-founder and the Plan to Part was created.

Services We Do

Peace of Mind

Planning your arrangements now will ease your family's stress and financial burden during an emotional period. You can take comfort knowing you have removed this anxiety during this very emotional time.

Be in Control

By taking control of your final wishes you can be certain to have a comprehensive plan prepared and ready to be implemented. This will ensure your estate is handled by you in detail when the time comes.

Designees

Empower your designees to make sure your plans are administered with your preferences for senior living, healthcare, investments, pets and other important matters.

Personal possessions and memories

You can avoid family conflict and duress by having your personal items recorded and designated now. Having open conversations and soliciting input with loved ones is a great way to get ahead of conflicts down the road. It can bring you peace knowing these items will bring joy to your chosen inheritors.

Final wishes and instructions

We provide an easy app that will guide you through the daunting task of organizing an end of life plan. Your end of life instructions, passwords memories and communications will be stored for your family. A priceless gift for family and loved ones.

Financial legacy and other assets.

Plan to Part stores your financial information for when the time comes and also offers advice on how to finance end of life plans. In addition, our affiliated estate planners can offer valuable guidance on how to transfer assets without paying hefty taxes and fees.
